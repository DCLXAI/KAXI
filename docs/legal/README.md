## Operating Rules

1. The AI may provide official-source summaries, checklists, risk flags, and preparation guidance.
2. The startup may operate software, school search, cost comparison, intake, scheduling, and partner routing.
3. Case-specific visa judgment, official filing strategy, administrative-agency submission document preparation, proxy submission, appeal representation, and paid administrative-scrivener work must be performed only by a licensed administrative-scrivener partner under a separate engagement.
4. The product must not imply visa approval guarantees, illegal job placement, fake documents, or avoidance of immigration rules.
5. Any user-facing legal/privacy text must keep an explicit draft/legal-review status until counsel approval.
